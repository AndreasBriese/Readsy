Bookmarklet is here: Readsy. Drag it onto your favorites bar and click it whenever you're reading a tedious article!

I am a sophomore at Princeton University majoring in Computer Science.

This is a little experiment to make it easier to read longform content. The backend is made using Flask, and uses APIs and modules from Readability, BeautifulSoup, PDFMiner, Requests, and more. The front end uses Bootstrap 3, HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery.
